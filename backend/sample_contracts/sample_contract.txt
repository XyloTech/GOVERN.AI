PARTIES:
This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between:

Party B: DataServices LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of California

SERVICES:
Party B agrees to provide cloud computing services and data management solutions to Party A for the duration of this Agreement.

CONTRACT VALUE:
The total contract value is $500,000.00 USD, payable in quarterly installments of $125,000.00 USD.

TERM AND RENEWAL:
This Agreement shall commence on January 1, 2024 and shall continue until December 31, 2024, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions herein. OBLIGATIONS:
1. Party B shall provide 99.9% uptime guarantee for all services.
2. Party A shall make payments within 30 days of invoice receipt.
3. Both parties shall maintain appropriate data security measures in compliance with GDPR and SOC 2 standards.

TERMINATION:
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice. In the event of material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately upon written notice.

PENALTIES:
In the event of service downtime exceeding 0.1% in any calendar month, Party B shall provide service credits equal to 10% of that month's payment.

RISK FACTORS:
- Data privacy compliance requirements (GDPR, SOC 2)
- Financial exposure: $500,000 annual commitment
- Service dependency risk
